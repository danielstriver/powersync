Our Solution — Four Integrated Pillars

① Decentralized Infrastructure

50 kW solar array with 100 kWh lithium battery bank. Operates fully independent of the national grid, ensuring uninterrupted power during outages.

② Seamless Digital Payments

Purchase electricity credit instantly via MTN Mobile Money or Airtel Money — no token codes, no manual entry, no bank account required.

③ Smart IoT Metering

Real-time consumption tracking, automatic load management, and 24/7 fault detection with instant alerts to the management platform.

④ Intelligent Management Platform

Centralized control that balances energy across all users, prioritizes critical loads, and provides operator dashboards with live analytics.

Proof of Concept — 2024 Kigali Pilot

  • 400 households served across the pilot community
  • 98.3% system uptime maintained throughout the entire pilot
  • Payment-to-power activation in under 5 seconds
  • 60% reduction in household energy costs vs. diesel generators
  • 91% of load demand met without grid dependency
  • Rwanda Energy Group (REG) compliant smart metering infrastructure
  • Pay-as-you-go — no lock-in contracts, no hidden fees

Impact & Vision

PowerSync directly supports Rwanda's Vision 2050 goal of universal electricity access. Starting with peri-urban communities, the platform scales across rural Rwanda and East Africa — delivering reliable, affordable, clean energy to millions still underserved by the national grid. Each deployed microgrid is fully community-owned and REG-compliant.
